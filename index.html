Filmmaker • Educator • Storyteller

Sinéad Walsh

Sinéad Walsh tells visually rich stories. A graduate of Trinity College, MA Art History & English, INA Paris & RTÉ Screen Ireland Producer Director training.

Sinéad's work includes working with children and persons with disabilities for organisations including Centre For Independent Living, Dublin Bus & Irish Rail.

Sinéad also works with children with disadvantaged backgrounds documenting life on the ground in school & local community settings using observational narrative style as seen here in Forest Time.

Sinéad's most recent feature documentary A Visual Storm recently screened at Irish Film Institute 2026 & Galway International Film Fleadh 2025.

Watch A Visual Storm - Aurelio Caminati

This film emerged from the need to bring to public attention the artwork of Italian avantgarde artist Aurelio Caminati, revealing his revitalising take on Irish landscape painting.
